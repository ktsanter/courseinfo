Accordion example

  • Be curious! Log in every weekday. Come to your online course at least as often as you come to your face-to-face classes
  • Follow the pacing guide. Don't get too far behind.
  • Read the class announcements every day.
  • Read messages, assignment feedback, and discussion replies every day.
  • Please contact me when you need help or if you have any questions. Your success is my first priority.
  • If you will be absent from class for more than two or three days, please let me know.
  • Abide by the Michigan Virtual academic honesty polices.
  • I will always treat you with respect and friendliness.
  • I will always encourage your learning and growth
  • I will reply to your messages/texts/emails withn 24 hours (not counting weekends)
  • I will grade your work within 3 days (not counting weekends)
  • I will communicate with your mentor and parents regarding your progress in class
You have a lot of options for contacting me and I'm always open to suggestions for other methods. I look forward to hearing from you!
  • Message Instructor: You can find a link to this in the navigation bar on the left-hand side of your Blackboard page. It's basically an email system built into Blackboard
  • voice/text: Call me at 734.999.0587. This is a Google Voice number I have set up just for students
  • email: you can reach me at ksanter.michiganvirtual.org
  • office hours: I use a tool called Adobe Connect to hold "live" office hours, typically twice a week. We can also make an appointment if the times don't suit you. Click on the Live Chat option in the navigation bar to check it out
There is no final exam or project for Digital Literacy.
